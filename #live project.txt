#live project

The LIVE project represents a form of haptic interface. Touch is an essential part of recreating a past event. Hitchcock uses Sarah to illustrate how haptic interface could be used in order to get a feel of the prison she stayed in. 

Hitchcock, Tim. "Big Data for Dead People: Digital Readings and the Conundrums of Positivism".Historyonics. Published December 9,2013.
