#Links

With the introduction of the internet, data is now more accessible than ever. The current problem is not the lack of resources but rather the lack of links. In order to piece together a complete picture of Sarah's life a plethora of databases had to be accessed. This meant that Hitchcock as a historian had to be versed in the online tools available. By linking together all of the information that was availble Hitchcock was able to create a background for Sarah.

Hitchcock, Tim. "Big Data for Dead People: Digital Readings and the Conundrums of Positivism".Historyonics. Published December 9,2013.
